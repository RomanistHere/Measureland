Measureland support

How to get it

Right now we don’t have a fancy form, which you could fill with your query, it’s probably on its way. So for now we suggest you tell us your story directly via email or leave your feedback/question/suggestion in our GitHub repository. But before you do, check our FAQ just below.

FAQ

  • "Too many attempts" or "You rate too often" - check how to resolve these issues.
  • I can’t register because there is no email with verification or server is unavailable/slow - Measureland is in sort of beta testing stage right now, means we don’t expect everything to work perfect and stable, some of the third-party providers (DigitalOcean, MailGun and so on) are not configured for handling a lot of people, bu it’s gonna be one day. Don’t forget that all the work is mostly done by a single guy at this stage - so your contributing would matter, We would appreciate it :)
  • Something specific isn’t working, like map is not moving or zooming, register popup is not opening and so on - to be extremely useful your report should include key points like:
    • your browser and OS (example: “Firefox, Windows laptop”);
    • description of the problem itself (example: “Register popup don’t open”);
    • if the problem persists (after reload), what are the steps to reproduce it - ideally with screenshots or video (example: “click side menu at the right top corner → click on the login button → click “I want to register” button → nothing happens)

Our project is open-source, you can leave your questions or suggestions in the GitHub repository or simply mail it: RomanistHere@pm.me
