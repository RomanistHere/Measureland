Measureland Constitution. Part III. Paid options.

We believe in a social hierarchy-less society.

We will try to form a little world, where it doesn't matter if you have money or not. You will have access to everything anyway. And will try to combine it with making some money to support Measureland itself.

Most things below are in the development stage. Contact us directly to leave a request. Going to be automated soon!

What you can pay for.

  • Regions of interest. Pick a certain area and we will notify (weekly/monthly) about any changes inside.
    Starting 2$ per week.
  • Place recommendations. We can provide you with the best places in town/area to move in or have a few-night stand based on user reviews.
    Starting 5$ per recommendation.
  • Donate. Project’s built and paid for by the solo guy, RomanistHere (Roman Smunyov). It’s intended to help people to pick a nice place to move in or stay at without actually visiting it or doing any additional research.
    I didn’t make this service profit-oriented. I don’t plan to monetize it with annoying ads and so on. All I tried was to solve a problem and help people. Nevertheless, money is a motivation to continue, as well as an opportunity to make further developments full time - I’d like that and I’d appreciate your help a lot. #Crypto.
  • Future unknown. We have a lot of ideas! Follow the news and you won't miss something useful for yourself :)

Right now we don't have it automated. So please, send us an email if you're interested in making payments.

Our project is open-source, you can leave your questions or suggestions in the GitHub repository or simply mail it: RomanistHere@pm.me
